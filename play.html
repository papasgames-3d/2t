Monkey Mart Game's Overview

Welcome to Monkey Mart - the exciting store management game where you become a clever monkey running your own shop! Experience the perfect blend of casual gameplay and strategic management as you build your retail empire from the ground up.

Development Information

Developer: The game is created by independent developer Tiny Dobbins.

Platform: The game is available on Web browsers, Android, and IOS, very convenient to experience at any time.

Developed with cutting-edge web technologies, Monkey Mart combines smooth gameplay mechanics with charming visuals. Regular updates ensure fresh content and improved features for players worldwide.

Game Features

  • Intuitive store management system
  • Diverse customer interactions
  • Progressive upgrade mechanics
  • Engaging achievement system
  • Regular content updates

How to Play Monkey Mart

Master the art of store management with these simple controls:

  • Left Click: Select and move items
  • Right Click: Cancel selection
  • Mouse Movement: Navigate your character

Success Tips:

  • Keep your store organized
  • Prioritize customer satisfaction
  • Manage inventory efficiently
  • Plan strategic upgrades
  • Maintain store cleanliness
