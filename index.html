Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start playing Monkey Mart?

Simply click the "Play Now" button above to start your Monkey Mart adventure. The game runs directly in your browser with no downloads required!

What are the best strategies for beginners?

Start by focusing on basic products, keep your store clean, and gradually expand. Pay attention to customer needs and always maintain adequate stock levels.

How do I manage multiple departments efficiently?

Train specialized monkeys for each department, optimize your store layout for smooth traffic flow, and monitor customer patterns to predict demand.

Can I save my progress?

Yes! Your progress is automatically saved in your browser. For the best experience, use the same device and browser consistently.
