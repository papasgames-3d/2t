What to Expect in Level 3

  • New products and machines (dairy products, advanced processing)
  • Larger supermarket space to manage
  • Increased customer flow and demands
  • Additional helper characters to assist you

Essential Strategies

1. Stock Management

  • Prioritize high-demand items
  • Keep shelves consistently stocked
  • Upgrade storage capacity early
  • Organize inventory efficiently

2. Customer Service

  • Maintain quick checkout times
  • Add multiple checkout counters
  • Monitor satisfaction levels
  • Keep popular items easily accessible

3. Helper Management

  • Strategic helper placement
  • Rotate helpers based on demand
  • Prioritize key areas
  • Maintain efficient workflows

Pro Tips and Tricks

Upgrade Strategically

Focus on upgrading high-traffic areas and essential equipment first.

Space Management

Organize your layout efficiently to minimize walking distances.

Production Balance

Maintain optimal production rates to match customer demand.

❌ Poor helper assignment
